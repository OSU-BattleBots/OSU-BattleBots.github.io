About the Battle Bots Club

Battle Bots is a new club dedicated to bringing BattleBots back to oregon. Our main goal is to help people build all classes of Battle bots from 1/3 lbs all the way up to the 120 lbs battlebots used on the tv show.

Our goal for this year is to only compete in the 1/3 lbs to 3 lbs classes. We are currently in the process of building 1 lbs plastics to get all our members familiar with battle bots.

Competition

We will be hosting a competition at least once a term for Beetle weights and lower. This competition will be hosted at the beginning of dead week each term.

Everyone is welcome, We hope those in washinton idaho and califonia can all join us for our coming competitions. Our competition Dates are listed below.

Ant Weight

1 lbs

We will be running a 1lbs plastic weight class and a normal 1lbs class if we have enough robots. Rules can be found under the rules tab as for the plastic weights here are the specific rules:

  • PET, PETG, ABS, or PLA, PLA+ are the only materials that can be used for the chassis and weapons. No other types of plastics or materials allowed (ie. metal, carbon fiber, UHMW, etc)
  • ---
  • Motors, electronics, axles, fasteners and adhesives can be any material, but cannot be used in such a way to enhance the structural integrity, armor the robot, or enhance any weapon.

Robot may be disqualified at the Event Organizer’s discretion if it is deemed to violate the spirit of the class. Contact the event organizer ahead of time if you are not sure your robot meets the above definition.
